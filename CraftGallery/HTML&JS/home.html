why you should learn with us?

 
    Our philosophy is to find stillness in the pottery process.
    All of our sessions are an opportunity to slow down and
    experience the therapeutic benefits that come with having
     your hands covered in clay.
    We tell all of our students & members to focus
     on the experience, rather than the end product as this 
     is what sets you up for a calming session, spent growing
     from mistakes and letting go of attachments & expectations.
