ABOUT US

We Are The First Pottery Gellary In KSA !


Craft Gallery is the first pottery Gellary in Saudi Arabia.

It is an open practice pottery Gallery in Riyadh, dedicated to providing learning opportunities and workshop space for anyone with a passion for working creatively with clay.

We are fully resourced with three electric kilns, a variety of high quality wheels and hand building tables, a slab roller; pug mill; spray booth; clay extruder, and tools. We also have design studio desks and business office space available.
