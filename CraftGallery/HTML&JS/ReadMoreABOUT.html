Gallery is an open practice pottery Gallery in Riyadh, dedicated to providing learning opportunities and workshop space for anyone with a passion for working creatively with clay.

Riyadh Craft Gallery Our open Gallery allows artists and makers to develop and refine their pottery practice. Based at Riyadh Front in Riyadh, the building is a creative arts hub in an old Victorian Primary School. We are surrounded by small cafes, bakeries and restaurants. We have both the bustling city and the tranquility . Our Gallery is friendly, supportive and fully resourced with three electric kilns, a variety of high quality wheels and hand building tables, a slab roller, pug mill, spray booth, clay extruder, and tools. We also have design studio desks and business office space available.

Our members have a wide variety of objectives - from exploring a creative passion through clay, to building careers in the creative industry as studio potters. You residency, or can also drop-in for practice sessions, join us on a short term attend one of our professional development classes. work in partnership with Princess Nourah bint Abdulrahman Universty
